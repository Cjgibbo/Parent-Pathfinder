A few quick questions to make sure you get the questionnaire that fits your situation. There are no wrong answers — every path leads somewhere useful.

Question 1 of 5
Has your child received a formal diagnosis from a doctor, psychiatrist, or psychologist?

A diagnosis given by a licensed professional — not a self-assessment or school observation alone.

Question 2 of 5
Which of the following describes your child's diagnosis?

Select all that apply. It's common for kids to have more than one.

Question 3 of 5
How much of a daily challenge is defiance in your home?

Arguing, refusing, pushing back on nearly everything — how central is this to your daily experience?

Question 4 of 5
Have you worked with any professional to help your child?

Therapy, psychiatry, medication, school services, ABA, occupational therapy, PCIT — anything counts.

Question 5 of 5
How long have you been navigating this?

From when you first knew something was different — not from when you got a diagnosis.
